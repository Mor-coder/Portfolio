Designing myself: an iterative process

Hi there, I'm a ux/ui designer with a keen interest in the human-machine relationship and a yoga teacher.

I have worked extensively on the integration of AI into workflows, specifically within the finance industry.

And I hold space for people to come and practice in a safe, friendly, and inclusive environment.

Funny enough, both processes - that is learning how to use digital technologies, and learning how to move in a yoga class - can equally be pretty daunting experiences, and it's my job, as the facilitator, to create an environment in which everyone feels free & safe to move in the digital, or physical, space.

How yoga has affected the way I think about UX and viceversa

  • Building empathy -

    I've noticed that both yoga and tech devices, seem to carry the stigma of being unreachable, too complicated, and to require some pre-requisites in order to be good at it. My role, in both cases, is to shift the perception and make people understand that there is no right or wrong way of doing things and to trust their intuition.
