Why UX?

I believe technological innovations have the power to support and empower people in their daily lives. However, without clear and intuitive guidance, technology can be confusing—or even counterproductive. I am passionate about UX because it sits at the intersection of humans and machines, and it is the key factor in making technology understandable and empowering for its users. I am naturally curious, with a strong interest in solving problems—especially those that push me outside my comfort zone, encouraging me to think differently, more broadly, and sometimes more boldly.

Here’s a selection of projects that, for one reason or another, challenged me to think creatively and explore new directions.
